# Agentic AI and the Next Intelligence Explosion (arXiv)

Surfaced via the alphaXiv / NLP-newsletter digest. (arXiv version of the essay also archived at [[agentic-ai-and-the-next-intelligence-explosion]].)

## Summary

This essay argues against the classic "singularity" vision of a single titanic mind bootstrapping itself to godlike intelligence. The authors contend that intelligence is high-dimensional and relational rather than a scalar quantity, and that — like previous major evolutionary transitions — the coming step-change in machine intelligence will be plural, social, and deeply entangled with humans. There is no clean "human scale" to surpass, because human intelligence is already a collective property rather than an individual one.

They observe sociality at two levels. Within ostensibly singular reasoning models, frontier systems (e.g., DeepSeek-R1, QwQ-32B) do not improve merely by "thinking longer"; they simulate multi-agent-like internal debates the authors call a **"society of thought"** — distinct cognitive perspectives that argue, question, verify, and reconcile, which causally accounts for accuracy gains on hard reasoning tasks. Strikingly this is emergent: RL rewarding only accuracy spontaneously increases multi-perspective conversational behavior. At the larger level, intelligence emerges from orchestrating societies of AI agents alongside humans in "centaur" configurations. The piece calls for importing a century of organizational and social-science knowledge (team size, hierarchy, role differentiation, network structure) into the design of multi-agent AI reasoning.

## Key points

- Rejects the single-superintelligence "singularity" framing; predicts a **plural, social, human-entangled** intelligence explosion.
- Intelligence is **relational and collective**, not a scalar that is simply more/less than "human scale."
- **"Society of thought"**: reasoning models (DeepSeek-R1, QwQ-32B) internally simulate multi-party debate; this conversational structure causally drives reasoning accuracy.
- The multi-perspective behavior is **emergent** — RL for accuracy alone elicits it, unprompted.
- Larger-scale intelligence comes from **"centaur" human+agent societies** orchestrated together.
- Advocates bringing organizational/social-science theory (team composition, hierarchy, roles, networks) to bear on AI reasoning design.
