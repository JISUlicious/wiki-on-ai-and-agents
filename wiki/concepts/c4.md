# C4 (Colossal Clean Crawled Corpus)

A ~750GB, ~156B-token English pre-training corpus derived from Common Crawl with quality filtering and deduplication. Released by [[google-brain|Google]] alongside [[t5-raffel-2019|T5]] (Raffel et al., 2019).

C4's filtering pipeline drops pages that don't end in terminal punctuation, contain explicit content (via a curated wordlist), have many list-like lines, or look like boilerplate. The result is one of the cleanest large-scale web text corpora released publicly.

C4 became a standard pre-training corpus across NLP from 2019 onward, used by many open-weight models. Successor variants — mC4 (multilingual), C4-200M, RedPajama, FineWeb — refine the filtering pipeline further but inherit the basic premise.
