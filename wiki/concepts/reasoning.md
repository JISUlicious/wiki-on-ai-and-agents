# Reasoning

Reasoning is the umbrella concept for multi-step inference in LLMs — deriving an answer through intermediate steps rather than producing it in one shot. It spans prompted [[chain-of-thought]], RL-trained reasoning models that learn to think before answering, and [[latent-reasoning]] performed in hidden states rather than text. Reasoning quality is a central axis along which modern models are differentiated.
