# Meta-Learning

Meta-learning, or "learning to learn," aims to improve the learning and adaptation process itself rather than just performance on a single task. By optimizing over distributions of tasks, a meta-learner acquires inductive biases that let it adapt quickly to new ones. It underlies [[self-improving-agent]] systems that revise their own strategies over time.
