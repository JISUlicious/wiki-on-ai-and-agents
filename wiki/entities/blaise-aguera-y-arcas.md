# Blaise Agüera y Arcas

Computer scientist; VP and Fellow at [[google-research|Google]]; leads Google's **Paradigms of Intelligence Team**. Previously at Microsoft (where he led the Bing maps / Photosynth / Seadragon team), and earlier a founder of the Seadragon technology that Microsoft acquired. Long-time public voice on AI, frequently writing essays connecting AI to biology, philosophy of mind, and social theory.

Co-author of "[[agentic-ai-and-the-next-intelligence-explosion]]" (2026).
