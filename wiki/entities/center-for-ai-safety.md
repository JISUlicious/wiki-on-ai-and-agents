# Center for AI Safety (CAIS)

US-based nonprofit AI safety research and advocacy organization, founded 2022 by [[dan-hendrycks|Dan Hendrycks]]. Produces both technical research ([[representation-engineering-zou-2023|RepE]], evaluations, red-teaming) and public policy work. Coordinated the May 2023 "Statement on AI Risk" — a one-sentence open letter ("Mitigating the risk of extinction from AI should be a global priority alongside other societal-scale risks such as pandemics and nuclear war") signed by leaders of OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Anthropic, and many academic AI researchers.
