# Google Research

Google's broader research organization, encompassing many specialized teams (Google AI, Google AI Language, Google Brain — though Brain was historically distinct). Several foundational ML papers in this wiki came out of Google Research or its sub-teams.

In 2023, [[google-brain]] merged with DeepMind to form Google DeepMind, and the Google Research / Google AI structure was reorganized.
