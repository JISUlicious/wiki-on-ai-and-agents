# Martin Wattenberg

Computer scientist; faculty at Harvard SEAS. Co-founder (with [[fernanda-viegas|Fernanda Viégas]]) of the Google PAIR (People + AI Research) initiative. Background in visualization (TensorFlow's TensorBoard, Embedding Projector) and interpretability — many papers on how to inspect and probe neural networks. Co-author of "[[inference-time-intervention-li-2023|ITI]]" (2023).
