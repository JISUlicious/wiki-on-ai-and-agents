# Dan Hendrycks

AI safety researcher; executive director of the [[center-for-ai-safety|Center for AI Safety (CAIS)]] (founded 2022). Senior author on "[[representation-engineering-zou-2023|Representation Engineering]]" (2023). Also created the [[mmlu|MMLU]] benchmark (Hendrycks et al. 2021), MATH benchmark, and many other widely-used evaluations of LLM safety and capability. Has been a major public voice on catastrophic AI risk; co-author of "An Overview of Catastrophic AI Risks" (2023) and led the May 2023 "Statement on AI Risk" signed by major AI lab leaders.
