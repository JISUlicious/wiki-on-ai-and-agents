**Microsoft** is a US tech conglomerate and one of the principal commercial backers of modern AI infrastructure. Its in-house research arm [[microsoft-research]] has authored notable agent / coding benchmarks and models, including [[swe-bench-live]] (a freshness-preserving extension of [[swe-bench]]) and the [[magma]] vision-language-action foundation model. Microsoft is also OpenAI's primary cloud and capital partner, shipping LLM-powered products through Azure OpenAI, Copilot, and GitHub Copilot.

- Parent of [[microsoft-research]]
- Major equity / cloud partner of [[openai]]
- Ships GitHub Copilot, Microsoft 365 Copilot, Azure OpenAI Service
- Research output spans [[llm-agent]], [[vision-language-action-model]], and benchmark design
