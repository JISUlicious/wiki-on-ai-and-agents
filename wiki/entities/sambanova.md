SambaNova Systems is an AI accelerator and platform company based in Palo Alto. Founded in 2017 by researchers from Stanford and Sun Microsystems, the company builds Reconfigurable Dataflow Unit (RDU) hardware and an associated full-stack platform for training and serving LLMs.

SambaNova has also contributed research, including co-authorship on [[agentic-context-engineering]], which studies how to structure long contexts for tool-using agents.

## Notable work

- [[agentic-context-engineering]] — collaboration on long-context agent design (see `agentic-context-engineering-zhang-2025`).
- SambaNova Cloud / SambaNova Suite — hosted inference + fine-tuning.
- RDU accelerators competing in the LLM-serving hardware space.
