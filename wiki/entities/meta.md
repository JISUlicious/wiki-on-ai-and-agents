Meta Platforms (formerly Facebook) is a US technology company whose ML research is conducted primarily through its research division [[fair]] (Fundamental AI Research, originally Facebook AI Research). This entity page covers the parent company; FAIR has its own page for lab-level research output.

Meta is the steward of the LLaMA open-weights model family — a major driver of the open-source LLM ecosystem — and contributed to [[gaia]] alongside [[huggingface]] and [[autogpt]].

## Notable work

- LLaMA model family (see [[llama]]) — open-weights base and instruct models.
- [[fair]] research output (RAG, BART, OPT, NLLB, etc.).
- Co-authorship of [[gaia]] (see [[gaia-mialon-2023]]).
- PyTorch (originally developed at FAIR).
