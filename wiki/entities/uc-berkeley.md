# UC Berkeley

University of California, Berkeley. The EECS department and the Sky Computing Lab (led by [[ion-stoica]]) are the main loci for LLM and agent-systems research relevant to this wiki. Berkeley is the origin institution for MemGPT, Sleep-time Compute, and a long line of systems/ML projects (Ray, vLLM, Gorilla, and many more).

The MemGPT group spun out as [[letta]] in 2024 to productize stateful agent runtimes.

## Notable work

- [[memgpt-packer-2023]] — virtual context management for LLM agents.
- [[sleep-time-compute-lin-2025]] — offline reasoning between agent turns.
- Spinout: [[letta]].
