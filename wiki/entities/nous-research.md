# Nous Research

Decentralized AI research collective. Known for the **Hermes** open-source LLM series (Hermes 1, 2, 3, ...) — typically fine-tunes of Llama and other open base models with a distinctive "uncensored, broadly capable assistant" personality. In 2025–2026 expanded into agent products: [[hermes-agent|Hermes Agent]] is the company's flagship agent system.
