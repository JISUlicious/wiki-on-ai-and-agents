# Alec Radford

Researcher at [[openai|OpenAI]]. First author of "[[gpt-1-radford-2018|Improving Language Understanding by Generative Pre-Training]]" (2018) and "Language Models are Unsupervised Multitask Learners" (2019, [[gpt-2-radford-2019|GPT-2 paper]]), and a major contributor to [[gpt-3]] and subsequent OpenAI work. Architect of the GPT recipe.

Earlier, Radford was at indico Data Solutions and was first author on the influential DCGAN paper (2015).
