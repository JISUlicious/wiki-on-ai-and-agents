# Dzmitry Bahdanau

Belarusian researcher; first author of "[[neural-machine-translation-bahdanau-2014|Neural Machine Translation by Jointly Learning to Align and Translate]]" (2014, ICLR 2015) — the paper that introduced the soft-[[attention-mechanism|attention]] mechanism. Then PhD student under [[yoshua-bengio]] at Université de Montréal (Mila), via Jacobs University Bremen. Later at Element AI / ServiceNow.
