# James Evans

Computational social scientist; faculty at the University of Chicago and external faculty at the Santa Fe Institute. Affiliated with [[google-research|Google]]'s Paradigms of Intelligence Team. Researches the sociology of science, knowledge, and collective intelligence — including how human and AI cognition interact in collaborative settings.

Lead author of "[[agentic-ai-and-the-next-intelligence-explosion]]" (2026).
