# PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC)

Global professional-services firm with an applied-AI / agent-systems research thread in its US arm. Authored research on long-horizon conversational memory ([[chronos]], 2026) and the harness-vs-retriever joint study [[grep-all-you-need-sen-2026]] that named [[agentic-search]] as a discipline.

## Notable work

- [[chronos]] — temporal-aware conversational agent with structured event retrieval for long-term memory (Sen et al. 2026).
- [[grep-all-you-need-sen-2026]] — "Is Grep All You Need? How Agent Harnesses Reshape Agentic Search" (Sen et al. 2026); systematic measurement of retriever × harness × tool-delivery on [[longmemeval]].

## People

- **Sahil Sen** — first author on both papers.
- **Elias Lumer** — co-author; also leads adjacent work on tool/agent selection in production.
- **Anmol Gulati**, **Vamse Kumar Subbiah**, **Akhil Kasturi** — co-authors across the PwC agentic-systems papers.
