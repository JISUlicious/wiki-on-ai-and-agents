# Tom B. Brown

Researcher; first author of "[[gpt-3-brown-2020|Language Models are Few-Shot Learners]]" (2020). At [[openai|OpenAI]] during the GPT-3 work; later moved to [[anthropic]] with several other GPT-3 co-authors (including [[dario-amodei]] and [[sam-mccandlish]]) when Anthropic was founded in 2021.
