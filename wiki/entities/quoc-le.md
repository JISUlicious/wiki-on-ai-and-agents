# Quoc Le

Vietnamese-Australian researcher at [[google-brain|Google Brain]]. Co-author of "[[sequence-to-sequence-learning-sutskever-2014|seq2seq]]" (2014). Long career in foundational deep learning at Google: Doc2Vec, AutoML / Neural Architecture Search, neural seq2seq for NMT, and many subsequent large-scale training efforts including PaLM and Gemini-era work.
