Microsoft Research (MSR) is the research arm of [[microsoft]], with labs in Redmond, Cambridge, Beijing, Asia, India, and elsewhere. It has a long history in NLP, systems, and ML, and in the LLM era has contributed both foundational research (e.g., DeBERTa, phi small-model line) and agentic benchmarks/models.

Recent agent-era contributions include [[swe-bench-live]] — a continuously updated SWE benchmark that resists training-data contamination — and [[magma]], a multimodal foundation model for embodied and GUI agents.

## Notable work

- [[swe-bench-live]] — live-updating SWE evaluation (see [[swe-bench-live-zhang-2025]]).
- [[magma]] — multimodal foundation model for agentic tasks (see [[magma-yang-2025]]).
- AutoGen agent framework.
- phi small-model series, DeBERTa, ORCA.
