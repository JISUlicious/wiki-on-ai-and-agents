# UC Santa Barbara

University of California, Santa Barbara — research university; its NLP / ML group (Shiyu Chang's lab) led the realistic agent-skills benchmark [[agentic-skills-in-the-wild-liu-2026]], in collaboration with [[mit|MIT]] CSAIL (Tommi Jaakkola) and the MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab.

## Notable work

- [[agentic-skills-in-the-wild-liu-2026]] — "How Well Do Agentic Skills Work in the Wild" (Liu et al. 2026); benchmarks [[agent-skills|skill]] usage when retrieving from ~34,000 real skills. See [[skill-evaluation]].
