# KyungHyun Cho

Korean researcher; co-author of "[[neural-machine-translation-bahdanau-2014|Bahdanau attention]]" (2014). Introduced the GRU (Gated Recurrent Unit, a simpler LSTM variant). Now faculty at NYU. Founding figure in neural machine translation.
