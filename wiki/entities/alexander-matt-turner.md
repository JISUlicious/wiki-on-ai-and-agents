# Alexander Matt Turner

AI safety / interpretability researcher; first author of "[[activation-engineering-turner-2023|Steering Language Models With Activation Engineering]]" (2023) — the canonical [[activation-addition|ActAdd]] paper — and co-author of [[contrastive-activation-addition-rimsky-2023|CAA]] (Rimsky 2023). Central figure in the [[steering]] literature. Affiliated with the Center for Human-Compatible AI; runs the "TurnTrout" blog. Previously did alignment-theory work on impact measures and power-seeking.
