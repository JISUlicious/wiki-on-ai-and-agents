# Ohio State University

Ohio State University (OSU). The NLP group led by Yu Su is the relevant locus for this wiki — known for retrieval-augmented generation, knowledge-graph reasoning, and neurobiologically inspired memory systems. The group produced HippoRAG (2024) and HippoRAG 2 (2025).
