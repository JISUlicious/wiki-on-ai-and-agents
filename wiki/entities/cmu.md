Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) is a private research university in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, with one of the longest-running and largest computer science / machine learning programs in the world. Its Language Technologies Institute and Machine Learning Department have produced foundational work in NLP, RL, robotics, and systems for ML.

CMU faculty (e.g., [[graham-neubig]]) contribute to the modern agent ecosystem through projects such as [[swe-gym]] and the OpenHands agent framework.

## Notable work

- [[swe-gym]] — executable SWE-agent training environment (see [[swe-gym-pan-2024]]).
- OpenHands / All Hands AI collaborations.
- Long history in NLP, RL (e.g., Tom Mitchell, NELL), and robotics.
