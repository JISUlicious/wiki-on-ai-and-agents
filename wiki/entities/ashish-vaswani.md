# Ashish Vaswani

Researcher and first-listed author of "[[attention-is-all-you-need-vaswani-2017|Attention Is All You Need]]" (2017), which introduced the [[transformer]]. At the time he was at [[google-brain]]. Per the paper's footnote, Vaswani — with Illia Polosukhin — designed and implemented the first Transformer models and was crucially involved in every aspect of the work.
