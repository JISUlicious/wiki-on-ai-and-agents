# Dimitris Papailiopoulos

Dimitris Papailiopoulos is a professor at UW-Madison and a senior co-author on several foundational looped-transformer papers. His work spans the expressivity of transformers as computational substrates (programmable computers) and their ability to learn iterative algorithms in-context.

- Senior co-author on [[looped-transformers-programmable-computers-giannou-2023]] — transformers as universal computers via looping.
- Senior co-author on [[looped-transformers-learning-algorithms-yang-2023]] — looped transformers learn data-driven iterative algorithms.
- UW-Madison; frequent collaborator with [[kangwook-lee]].
