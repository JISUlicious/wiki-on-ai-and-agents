# DeepMind (Google DeepMind)

UK-headquartered AI research lab, founded in London in 2010 by Demis Hassabis, Shane Legg, and Mustafa Suleyman. Acquired by Google in 2014. Famous for AlphaGo (2016), AlphaFold (2018, 2020), and a long line of reinforcement-learning and deep-learning research. Merged with [[google-brain|Google Brain]] in April 2023 to form **Google DeepMind**, the consolidated AI org behind the Gemini model family.

In this wiki, DeepMind is the origin of [[retro-borgeaud-2021|RETRO]] (2021) and Chinchilla (Hoffmann et al. 2022, cited under [[scaling-laws]]).
