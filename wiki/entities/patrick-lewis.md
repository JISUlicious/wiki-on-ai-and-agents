# Patrick Lewis

NLP researcher specializing in retrieval-augmented generation. Co-author of [[dpr-karpukhin-2020|DPR]] (2020) and **first author** of "[[rag-lewis-2020|Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP Tasks]]" (2020) — the paper that introduced the [[rag|RAG]] paradigm. At the time, he was at [[fair|Facebook AI Research]] / University College London.
