# Jan Leike

AI safety researcher; co-author of "[[rlhf-christiano-2017|Deep RL from Human Preferences]]" (2017) while at [[deepmind|DeepMind]]. Later at [[openai|OpenAI]] (2021–2024), where he co-led the Superalignment team with [[ilya-sutskever|Ilya Sutskever]]. Departed in May 2024 with public criticism of OpenAI's resource allocation toward safety; subsequently joined [[anthropic|Anthropic]].
