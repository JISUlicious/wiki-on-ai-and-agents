# Dario Amodei

AI researcher. Was VP of Research at [[openai|OpenAI]] during the [[gpt-2]] and [[gpt-3]] era — listed as a senior co-author on both papers. Left OpenAI in 2021 to co-found [[anthropic]] (along with his sister Daniela Amodei and other former OpenAI researchers), where he serves as CEO.

## Affiliations

- [[anthropic]] (co-founder & CEO, 2021–)
- [[openai]] (VP of Research, 2016–2020)
- Previously: Google Brain, Baidu Research
