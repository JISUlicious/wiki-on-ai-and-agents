# University of Toronto

Canadian research university with a deep historical connection to modern deep learning — Geoffrey Hinton's group there trained generations of researchers who went on to build the foundations of the field. In this wiki, Aidan N. Gomez was at U of T while contributing to the [[attention-is-all-you-need-vaswani-2017|Transformer paper]].
