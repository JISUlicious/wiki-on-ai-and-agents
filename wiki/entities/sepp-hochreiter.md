# Sepp Hochreiter

Austrian computer scientist; first author of "[[long-short-term-memory-hochreiter-1997|Long Short-Term Memory]]" (1997) with Jürgen Schmidhuber. Earlier diploma thesis (1991) was the original analysis of the [[vanishing-gradient-problem]] in recurrent networks. Currently heads the Institute for Machine Learning at JKU Linz; founded the LIT AI Lab there.
