# Christopher Manning

[[stanford-university|Stanford]] professor, director of Stanford NLP; co-author of "[[glove-pennington-2014|GloVe]]" (2014). Widely-used Speech and Language Processing textbook (with Schütze, then Jurafsky). Author of Stanford's NLP courses (CS224N) that trained a generation of NLP practitioners.
