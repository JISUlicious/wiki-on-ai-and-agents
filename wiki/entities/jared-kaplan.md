# Jared Kaplan

Theoretical physicist turned AI researcher. Faculty at Johns Hopkins University; was a co-author on [[gpt-3-brown-2020|GPT-3]] (2020). First author of "Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models" (Kaplan et al., 2020, arXiv 2001.08361), which established the predictive power-law relationships between model size, dataset size, compute, and loss that motivated the GPT-3 scale-up — see [[scaling-laws]]. Co-founded [[anthropic]] in 2021.
