# EleutherAI

Decentralized AI research collective, founded July 2020 on Discord and later incorporated as a nonprofit. Originally formed around the goal of replicating GPT-3-style models in the open — produced GPT-Neo, GPT-J, GPT-NeoX-20B, and the Pile dataset. Subsequently expanded into interpretability research, evaluation tooling (`lm-evaluation-harness` — the de-facto LLM evaluation framework), and multimodal work. In this wiki, EleutherAI is the affiliation of the [[sparse-autoencoders-cunningham-2023|Cunningham et al. SAE paper]] (2023) — one of the foundational interpretability works.
