# FAIR (Facebook AI Research / Meta AI)

Facebook's AI research lab, founded 2013 under Yann LeCun. Renamed **Meta AI** following Facebook's corporate rebrand to Meta in 2021, though "FAIR" persists as the research-org name. Major contributor to open-source ML — PyTorch, FastText, DETR, and (after the period covered by this wiki's early sources) the LLaMA family.

In this wiki, FAIR is the origin of [[dpr-karpukhin-2020|DPR]] and [[rag-lewis-2020|RAG]] — both seminal retrieval / retrieval-augmented-generation papers from 2020.
