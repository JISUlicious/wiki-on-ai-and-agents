# Jeff Dean

Google Senior Fellow; co-founder of [[google-brain|Google Brain]] (2011); foundational figure in Google's distributed-systems and ML infrastructure (MapReduce, BigTable, Spanner, TensorFlow). Co-author of the [[efficient-estimation-of-word-representations-mikolov-2013|original word2vec paper]] (2013) and many other large-scale ML systems papers. Since 2023, co-head of Google DeepMind alongside Demis Hassabis.
