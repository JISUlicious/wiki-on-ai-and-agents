# Tomas Mikolov

Czech computer scientist; first author of both **word2vec** papers — [[efficient-estimation-of-word-representations-mikolov-2013|"Efficient Estimation"]] (2013a) and [[distributed-representations-of-words-and-phrases-mikolov-2013|"Distributed Representations"]] (2013b). At [[google-brain|Google]] during this work; later at [[fair|Facebook AI Research]] and then CIIRC at the Czech Technical University.

word2vec made Mikolov one of the most-cited NLP researchers of the 2010s; the embedding paradigm he established is the conceptual ancestor of every later representation-learning system.
