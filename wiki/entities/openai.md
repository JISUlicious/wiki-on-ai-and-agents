# OpenAI

San Francisco-based AI research and deployment company, founded December 2015. In this wiki, OpenAI is the origin of the [[gpt-family|GPT family]] of decoder-only language models — [[gpt-1]] (2018), [[gpt-2]] (2019), [[gpt-3]] (2020), [[gpt-4]] (2023) — and the associated consumer product **ChatGPT** (2022).

Originally founded as a non-profit research lab focused on safe AGI; restructured in 2019 into a "capped-profit" company (OpenAI LP) under the non-profit OpenAI Inc. Major investments from Microsoft from 2019 onward.
