# MAI-Thinking-1

**MAI-Thinking-1** is a reasoning LLM from [[microsoft|Microsoft]] AI (MAI) — a **35B-active / 1T-total-parameter [[mixture-of-experts|Mixture-of-Experts]]** model. Surfaced via the alphaXiv weekly digest ([[mai-thinking-1-microsoft-2026]]).

## Distinctive design

- **"Hill-climbing machine"** — framed as a system for continuous, systematic self-improvement rather than a one-shot training run.
- **Human-data-only** — reportedly trained *exclusively on human-generated data*, a deliberate contrast with synthetic-data and self-distillation pipelines.
- Competitive on STEM reasoning, coding, and general tasks; emphasis on a helpfulness–safety balance. Part of Microsoft AI's mid-2026 "seven MAI models" wave.
