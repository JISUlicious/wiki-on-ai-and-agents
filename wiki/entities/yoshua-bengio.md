# Yoshua Bengio

Canadian computer scientist; 2018 Turing Award winner (shared with Geoffrey Hinton and Yann LeCun) for foundational work on deep learning. Director of Mila (Montreal Institute for Learning Algorithms). Co-author of "[[neural-machine-translation-bahdanau-2014|Bahdanau attention]]" (2014) — one of many foundational papers across word embeddings, NMT, GANs, attention, and more. More recently a vocal advocate for AI safety and governance.
