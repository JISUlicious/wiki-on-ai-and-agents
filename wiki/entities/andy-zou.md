# Andy Zou

AI safety researcher; CMU PhD student during the [[representation-engineering-zou-2023|RepE]] work (2023). First author of "Representation Engineering: A Top-Down Approach to AI Transparency" — the consolidating framework paper for the [[steering]] literature. Also known for early work on universal adversarial suffixes / jailbreaks (Zou et al. 2023, GCG).
