# Letta

Company founded in 2024 by [[charles-packer]], Sarah Wooders, and collaborators out of [[uc-berkeley]] to commercialize the MemGPT line of research. Letta produces an agent runtime where stateful, hierarchical memory (rather than a stateless prompt) is the central abstraction — agents are persistent processes with editable core memory, recall storage, and archival storage.

The company also published Sleep-time Compute (2025), which formalizes offline / "between-turn" reasoning passes as a first-class part of an agent's lifecycle.

## Notable work

- [[memgpt]] / [[memgpt-packer-2023]] — virtual-context-management agent architecture; the foundation of the Letta runtime.
- [[sleep-time-compute]] / [[sleep-time-compute-lin-2025]] — offline reasoning for long-lived agents.
