# Stanford University

US research university. Stanford NLP — directed by [[christopher-manning|Christopher Manning]] — is one of the most influential academic NLP labs of the 2010s–2020s. Origin of [[glove-pennington-2014|GloVe]] (Pennington, Socher, Manning 2014), Stanford CoreNLP, and the Stanford Sentiment Treebank, among many others.
