# Paul Christiano

AI safety researcher; first author of "[[rlhf-christiano-2017|Deep RL from Human Preferences]]" (2017), the foundational [[rlhf|RLHF]] paper. Previously at [[openai|OpenAI]]; later founded the Alignment Research Center (ARC) in 2021, where his team did the early "ARC evaluations" of frontier LLMs (the famous "GPT-4 hires a TaskRabbit worker" anecdote came from these). In 2023 joined the new US AI Safety Institute as head of AI safety.
