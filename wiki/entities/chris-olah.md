# Chris Olah

Researcher; founder of the modern [[mechanistic-interpretability|mechanistic interpretability]] research program. Previously at [[google-brain|Google Brain]] and [[openai|OpenAI]]; co-founder of [[anthropic|Anthropic]] (2021–). Editor of **Distill** (2017–2020), an interactive ML publication that hosted the original "Circuits" thread on interpreting vision models. Leads or has led the Transformer Circuits Thread at Anthropic.

In this wiki, Olah is a co-author of [[emotion-concepts-anthropic-2026|"Emotion Concepts and their Function in a Large Language Model"]] (2026).
