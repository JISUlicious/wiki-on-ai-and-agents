Hugging Face is an open-source ML company and the de-facto hub for sharing model weights, datasets, and evaluation tooling. Its libraries (`transformers`, `datasets`, `accelerate`, `peft`, `trl`) underpin a large fraction of academic and industrial LLM work, and the Hub hosts the majority of openly released model weights.

Beyond infrastructure, Hugging Face contributes original research, including co-authorship of [[gaia]], a benchmark for general-purpose AI assistants that emphasizes multi-step tool-using tasks.

## Notable work

- [[gaia]] — co-authored general-assistant benchmark (see [[gaia-mialon-2023]]).
- `transformers`, `datasets`, `trl` libraries.
- The Hugging Face Hub (models, datasets, Spaces).
- Open LLM Leaderboard and related evaluation infrastructure.
