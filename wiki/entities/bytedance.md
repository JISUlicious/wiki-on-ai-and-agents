ByteDance is a Chinese technology company best known internationally for TikTok and Douyin. Its research division, **ByteDance Seed**, has become a significant publisher of LLM and agent research, with output spanning multimodal models, reasoning systems, and GUI agents.

ByteDance Seed produced the UI-TARS line of [[computer-use-agent]] models, including UI-TARS-1 and the substantially upgraded [[ui-tars-2]], which couples a native multimodal model with sandboxed environment interaction for screen-based task automation.

## Notable work

- [[ui-tars-2]] — native GUI agent model (see [[ui-tars-2-wang-2025]]).
- UI-TARS-1 — initial release of the computer-use agent line.
- Broader Seed research on multimodal and reasoning LLMs.
