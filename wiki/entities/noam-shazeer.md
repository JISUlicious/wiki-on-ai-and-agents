# Noam Shazeer

Researcher and co-author of "[[attention-is-all-you-need-vaswani-2017|Attention Is All You Need]]" (2017). Per the paper's author-contribution footnote, Shazeer proposed [[scaled-dot-product-attention]], [[multi-head-attention]], and the parameter-free position representation — three of the most consequential design decisions in the [[transformer]] paper.

At the time of the Transformer paper, Shazeer was at [[google-brain]].
